About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Through partnerships with universities and industry organisations, IDR combines academic research with real-world projects to develop the next generation of digital risk professionals.

Our Model

🎓

Academy

Bootcamps and training programs that equip students and professionals with practical digital risk and cybersecurity skills.

💡

Innovation & Incubation

Developing future risk models, AI governance frameworks and digital risk research initiatives.

🛡️

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Who We Serve

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and industry practitioners across sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure seeking upskilling in cyber and digital risk.
